# Privacy Policy for Athi GPS Map Camera

**Last Updated:** June 28, 2026

Athi GPS Map Camera ("the App") is built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. We believe that your location and your photos are your business, not ours.

## 1. No Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. 
* **No Analytics:** We do not use any tracking tools (like Firebase Analytics or Mixpanel) to monitor your behavior.
* **No Personal Info:** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device identifiers.
* **No Cloud Storage:** All photos you take are saved locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

## 2. Device Permissions
The App requires specific permissions to function. Here is why:
* **Camera:** Used to display the viewfinder and capture images.
* **Location (GPS):** Used to calculate your current coordinates, altitude, and heading to stamp them onto your photos.
* **Storage/Media:** Used to save the final processed images to your device's gallery.
* **Internet:** Used solely to fetch map tiles from OpenStreetMap and to resolve your coordinates into a street address (Geocoding).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App uses **OSMdroid** and **OpenStreetMap** to display maps. 
* When the map is displayed, your device requests map "tiles" from OpenStreetMap servers. 
* This process involves sharing your IP address with the tile provider (a standard part of how the internet works), but no personal data or location history is stored or linked to you by the App.

## 4. No Monetization
* **No Ads:** The App is 100% ad-free.
* **No Sales:** We do not sell any data to third parties.
* **No In-App Purchases:** All features are available without payment.

## 5. Security
Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of your personal information being leaked from our servers. Your photos and location data remain under your physical control on your Android device.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository where this app is hosted.
